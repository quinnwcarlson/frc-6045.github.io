<p>First, open <a href="howtoopenelastic.html" class="btn">Elastic.</a><br>
        Depending on the settings, there are two ways to select the auto. Either way, first <a href="howtoconnecttotherobot.html" class="btn">connnect to the robot</a>. Then, follow the directions that correspond to how the auto selector looks.</p>
        <p id="passvscode">If the auto selector looks like this:<br>
        <img src="assets/images/howtoselectautos/1.png" alt="image 1"><br>
        Click the arrow and select the auto that you would like to run.<br>
        If the auto selector looks like this:<br>
        <img src="assets/images/howtoselectautos/2.png" alt="image 2"><br>
        Click on the auto that you would like to run.</p>
