<p>The technician is the team member who sets up the Drive Station (software) on the Drive Station (computer) at the Drive Station (where the drivers stand).<br>
    The technician must ensure that they locate the correct Drive Station (where the drivers stand).<br>
    If one is a technician, one should do these things after one arrives at the correct location:<br><br>
    Plug in the computer<br>
    Plug in the Ethernet cord to the computer<br>
    <a href="https://docs.wpilib.org/en/stable/docs/software/basic-programming/joystick.html#driver-station-joysticks" target="_blank" class="btn">Ensure that the controllers are connected to the correct USB ports</a><br>
    Once the robot is connected to the computer, <a href="howtoselectautos.html" class="btn">select an auto</a></p>
