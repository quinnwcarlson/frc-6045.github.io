---
title: "How to Update Motors"
order: 8
---

<p>First, open <a href="https://docs.revrobotics.com/rev-hardware-client/gs/install" target="_blank" class="btn">REV Hardware Client</a>. Plug the computer into a motor on the robot. Click on the motor that you would like to update.<br>
        <img src="assets/images/howtoupdatemotors/1.png" alt="image 1"><br>
        Click the "Update" tab.<br>
        <img src="assets/images/howtoupdatemotors/2.png" alt="image 2"><br>
        Select the version you wish the motors to be at (usually the latest version) and click the button below.<br>
        It may say something different than shown; in the case of the image, the motors were already up-to-date.
    </p>
