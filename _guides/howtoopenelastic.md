<p>To open Elastic for the first time, you will need <a href="https://docs.wpilib.org/en/stable/docs/zero-to-robot/step-2/wpilib-setup.html" target="_blank" class="btn">WPILib VS Code</a>. After you have opened WPILib VS Code, click the WPILib button at the top right of the editor.<br>
        <img src="assets/images/howtoopenelastic/1.png" alt="image 1"><br>
        Click the line that says “WPILib: Start Tool”.<br>
        <img src="assets/images/howtoopenelastic/2.png" alt="image 2"><br>
        Click on “Elastic”.<br>
        <img src="assets/images/howtoopenelastic/3.png" alt="image 3"><br></p>
