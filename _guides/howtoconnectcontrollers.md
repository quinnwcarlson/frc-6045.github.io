<p>After connecting a USB cable between the controller and the computer, open FRC Driver Station and navigate to the USB Devices Tab.<br>
    <img src="assets/images/howtoconnectcontrollers/1.png" alt="image 1"><br>
    Any controllers that are connected will have green text. In the picture, they both show "Controller (Xbox One For".<br>
    When a button is pressed on a controller, the associated controller name will be highlighted green.<br>
    Drag the controller names to reorder them.<br><br>

    Controllers must be connected by USB for use at competitions; wireless connections are not allowed.<br><br>
    
    For FRC 6045, the black driver controller should be in the "0" position and the white operator controller should be in the "1" position.</p>
