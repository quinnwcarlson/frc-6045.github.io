Technician Checklist

The technician is the team member who sets up the Drive Station (software) on the Drive Station (computer) at the Drive Station (where the drivers stand).
The technician must ensure that they locate the correct Drive Station (where the drivers stand).
If one is a technician, one should do these things after one arrives at the correct location:

Plug in the computer
Plug in the Ethernet cord to the computer
Ensure that the controllers are connected to the correct USB ports
Once the robot is connected to the computer, select an auto
